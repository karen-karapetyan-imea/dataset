Olga Hotujac

United States

View Profile

Artist Olga Hotujac’s contemporary abstract paintings feature the bold use of color and texture. Working in oil and using a pallet knife, Hotujac finds inspiration from the nature, building heavy textures of color and shape that seamlessly blend into the paintings structure. She has developed a unique brush stroke that is instantly recognizable, and her paintings can be found in private art collections around the world.
